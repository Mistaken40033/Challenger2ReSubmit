Hey there, fellow humans!

Gather 'round, because it's time to meet your friendly neighborhood dork, aka me! I go by Rabbit, and I'm just your average human-shaped bundle of quirks and interests.

First off, let's talk about my obsession with doodling, painting, and making stuff on canvas. Yep, I'm that person who gets lost in the world of colors and shapes, armed with a paintbrush and an overactive imagination. I mean, who needs Netflix when you can create your own epic sagas on a canvas, am I right?

Now, when I'm not lost in my artsy-fartsy world, you'll probably find me buried under a mountain of planners and to-do lists. Seriously, I plan my plans! But hey, it keeps my chaotic brain in check and helps me conquer the day like a boss.

Oh, and speaking of bosses, let's talk about my superpower: connecting with people! I've got this knack for befriending anyone and everyone. You name it, I'll strike up a conversation about it, from quantum physics to the best way to cook spaghetti. Let's be friends!

But wait, there's more! When I'm not busy being a creative genius or a social butterfly, you can bet I'm chilling with my fluffy sidekicks—my dogs—and my awesome hubby. They're my partners in crime, my cheerleaders, and my snuggle buddies all rolled into one.

And guess what? Despite my dorky tendencies, I'm finally adulting like a pro by pursuing a degree in something I never knew I'd love so much. Life's full of surprises, huh?

So there you have it, folks! I may be a certified dork, but I wear that badge with pride. Thanks for letting me geek out with you for a bit. Let's make some memories together!

Geeky high-fives, - Rabbit
